Sugar Cookies

December 25, 2022

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 5/8 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract

Tools

  • Electric mixer
  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper or silicon baking mats
  • Medium bowl
  • Large bowl
  • Rolling pin
  • Cookie cutters

Instructions

Make the dough and chill the dough

  1. In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking powder and salt.
  2. In a large bowl, mix butter and sugar with an electric mixer at high speed until smooth and creamy (about 2 minutes).
  3. Add the egg, vanilla extract and almond extract. Mix at high speed until mixed (about 1 minute).
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix on low speed until combined.
  5. Note: If the dough is too sticky and soft for rolling, add a tablespoon of flour.
  6. Half the dough equally and place each half on a parchment paper or a silicon baking mat.
  7. With the rolling pin, roll out the dough evenly to about 0.5 cm thickness.
  8. Cover the dough with a plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  9. Note: The dough can be refrigerated up to 2 days.

Cut out cookie shapes and bake the cookies

  1. Once the dough is chilled, preheat the oven to 350F.
  2. Use the cookie cutters to cut out cookie shapes from the dough.
  3. Re-roll the leftover dough and make more shapes until there is no more leftovers.
  4. Place a parchment paper or silicon baking mat on a baking sheet.
  5. On the parchment paper or silicon baking mat, arrange the cookies to be about 3 cm apart.
  6. Bake the cookies for about 11 - 13 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes and then transfer them to a cooling rack to let them completely cool.
